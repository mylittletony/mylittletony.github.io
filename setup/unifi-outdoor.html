Here's what you'll receive:

  • Instruction Card
  • UniFi Outdoor AP
  • Antennas
  • PoE injector and power cable
  • Wall mounting accessories, including screws

Here's some stuff you'll need:

  • Power socket
  • A working broadband connection
  • Your modem connected to your working broadband connection
  • Cucumber Tony account
  • 2 X Ethernet Cables
  • Switch (optional, if you need additional ports)

Plugging it all in

Plug the power cable into the PoE injector, and then connect it to a power socket.

Then connect an Ethernet Cable between your PoE injectorâ€™s LAN port, and a LAN port on your modem.

Next, connect your second Ethernet Cable between the PoE port on the PoE injector and the main port on your UniFi Outdoor AP.

When you've connecting everything up, the set up should look something like the image below. Your UniFi Outdoor AP will light up once the power is turned on and everything is plugged it.

Congrats! Now you just need to head into your Cucumber Tony dashboard and add in your lovely new box, click here to get started!

If you only have one spare port at the back of your modem and need extra ports for other devices, it is advised to buy a switch.

WARNING

Please use the power supply provided to you. An incorrect power supply may cause the fuse in the box to blow, breaking your box and warranty.
