Basic Guest WiFi Config

Creating an SSID

To set yourself up a public network for your guests, head into your location in Cucumber Tony and select SSIDs on the left hand sidebar.

Now click on Create Network. This will open up some new fields for you to populate. Give your new SSID a name, and then select Open and Splash Page from the options below to make a public network.

Creating a Splash Page

Now click on Splash Pages on the left hand side, and click on the Edit button under your newly created splash page.

From here, you can change your primary access type by selecting an option from the drop down menu. For the most basic option select Clickthrough and save your changes.

There are a number of other more advanced options here that will be covered in later sections.
