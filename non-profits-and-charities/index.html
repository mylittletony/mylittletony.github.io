Cucummber WiFi - Non-Profits and Education

It's nice to support people and companies in the world who are doing good things.
We also feel good supporting schools and education programs.

What you get

You get 40% off the monthly plans and 30% off the yearly ones (because it's already discounted).

You get all the features of the real plans. No ifs or buts. Pretty much the only condition of use, aside from our standard terms, is that you don't resell your plan to anyone else. It's not supposed to be there to make you money, the discount is to help you.

You need to be a not-for-profit and have all the correct certificates etc.

How do I get it?

To be considered, you need to send us a few bits of information. We need all of it.

  • Your account ID - it's in your settings in your Cucumber dash
  • The registered or legal name of your organisation
  • Your official website
  • A brief bio, up to 5 lines. No essays. What are you doing that's so good?
  • How many sites you'll need and how many access points you're intending on adding
  • Non-profit and charitable status certificates
  • Documentation supporting your educational status if you're a school

By submitting an application, you agree to the following.

I agree that my organisation:

  • is non-governmental, non-academic, non-commercial, and non-political
  • does not discriminate on the basis of gender, race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, political affiliation or beliefs, sexual orientation, or gender identity or expression
  • does not promote a particular religious affiliation, dogma, or doctrine as part of its mission or in exchange for its services

Once we've received your application, we'll process and apply within a few hours usually.

Common Questions

What do I get

Exactly what you would on the regular plans. You can select from any of the plans that fit your need. We'll apply the discount to your account.

We've already paid for some months. Can I get a refund?

Sadly no, if you're on a paid plan, your discount will be applied on the next billing cycle.

How can I prove nonprofit or charity status certification?

In the UK we'll need UK Gov. registration certificate. In the US we'll need a letter from the IRS showing your 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status or equivalent.

What if I get rejected?

There are no appeals and our decision is final. We reserve the right to grant or deny an organisation's application or participation at any time, for any reason, and to supplement or amend our eligibility guidelines at any time.
